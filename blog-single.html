Why Study MBBS in Russia?

Russia has been one of the top destinations for MBBS in Russia for Indian students for more than a decade now. Standing at the prominent 8th position in the world in terms of the quality of medical education, Russia has been home to thousands of Indian MBBS students who are working as renowned and well-established doctors today. As India and Russia share a cordial and friendly relationship, the government of the Russian Federation offers subsidized medical education to Indian students. The affordability of MBBS in Russia encourages many Indian medical aspirants to pursue their dreams in this largest country in the world which is home to more than 25 top medical universities around the globe.

The top medical universities in Russia have world-class infrastructures to support their high-quality medical education and comfortable accommodation facilities for Indian students. At present, there are more than 10,000 Indian students of MBBS in Russia chasing their dream careers to become excellent global doctors.

About Russia

Here are some interesting facts about the largest country in the world, Russia:

  • * Moscow is the capital of Russia.
  • * The official language of Russia is Russian.
  • * Almost 30% of the Russian population speak good English.
  • * The official currency of Russia is the Russian Ruble.
  • * There is a gap of 2 hours and 30 minutes between Indian and Russian time.
  • * Russia is the most developed country in the world.
  • * The country occupies 1/8th area of the earth.
  • * Russia is one of the independent countries in Eurasia.

Features:

  1. Medical universities in Russia are recognized by the National Medical Commission (erstwhile Medical Council of India).
  2. The tuition fees for MBBS in Russia are affordable, starting at just INR 3-4 lakhs a year.
  3. The complete medical education in Russia is taught in English to Indian students.
  4. Hassle-free admission process without any entrance exams like IELTS and TOEFL.
  5. Availability of comfortable hostels with round-the-clock basic amenities and hygienic Indian mess facilities.
  6. No donations or capitation fees.
  7. Affordable cost of living in Russia.
  8. Russian language is taught during the MBBS in Russia course for smooth communication with Russian patients.
  9. State-of-the-art infrastructure such as advanced classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, robotic learning, and practical teaching techniques.
  10. Indian medical students get practical exposure in affiliated world universities and hospitals.
  11. Russian medical universities have reputed faculty members including research analysts, doctors of science, and scientists.
  12. Top medical universities in Russia offer various specializations to Indian students.
  13. Russia is a safe country for Indian students of MBBS in Russia as the universities have strong and well-developed security systems.
  14. FMGE/NExT coaching by the best Indian doctors and professors is provided to the students at Russian medical universities.

Duration of MBBS Course in Russia

Following the guidelines of the National Medical Commission, the duration of an MBBS course in any foreign medical institution should not exceed that of an MBBS in India. In Russia, the total duration to complete an MBBS is 6 years, inclusive of a 12-month internship at prestigious hospitals. The initial 5 years are dedicated to theoretical and practical medical studies at universities, with the final year emphasizing clinical internships at leading hospitals in Russia.

Medium of Teaching in Russia

The top medical universities offering courses of MBBS in Russia have English as the medium of instruction for Indian and other international students.

Intake for MBBS Study in Russia
  • Every academic year in the medical universities of Russia comprises two semesters.
  • The first semester ranges from September to January and the second semester starts from February up till June.
  • The documentation process for admission begins from April and continues till October.
  • The teaching methodologies for MBBS in Russia are contemporary and practical.
  • MBBS courses in Russia are instructed in bilingual languages, especially English.
  • The highly qualified faculties of Russian medical universities have a detailed eye on the practical skill development of the students.
  • No donation and covert charges are asked from the students at the time of admissions.
Process of MBBS Admission in Russia
  • Step 1: Choose the best university for your MBBS in Russia after personalized counseling from Rus Education.
  • Step 2: Fill up the admission form for MBBS in Russia of the university and submit the required documents.
  • Step 3: Get admission/invitation letter from the university.
  • Step 4: Apply for the student visa by submitting passport and other supporting documents.
  • Step 5: Pay your tuition fees for the first year. If availing an education loan, Rus Education will take you through the process.
  • Step 6: Pack your bags with all the essential items that you will require in Russia.
  • Step 7: Board your flights to Russia and begin your journey of MBBS in Russia.
Documents Required
  • Duly filled-in Admission form
  • Scanned copy of Class 12th Pass Certificate/Mark sheet
  • Scanned copy of Class 10th Pass Certificate
  • NEET Result/Admit Card
  • Scanned copy of Passport (first and last page)
  • ID Proof (Aadhar, PAN, Driving License, etc.) if Passport is not ready at the time of application
  • Medical report with HIV report at the time of Visa stamping
